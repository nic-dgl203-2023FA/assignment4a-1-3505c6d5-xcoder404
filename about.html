About Apples

Apples are beloved fruits that come in various colors and flavors. They're not only tasty snacks but also key ingredients in pies, salads, and drinks. Apples are rich in fiber and vital nutrients, making them a healthy choice. Whether you enjoy them fresh or in a homemade pie, apples have a special place in our food traditions and hearts.

Red Delicious

Classic and Crisp

Red Delicious apples are known for their vibrant red color and a sweet, mild flavor, making them a favorite snack.

Golden Delicious

Mellow and Smooth

Golden Delicious apples offer a mellow, slightly sweet taste and a smooth texture, perfect for various culinary uses.

Granny Smith

Tart and Green

Granny Smith apples are famous for their tartness and green skin, ideal for baking, snacking, and salads.

Fuji

Sweet and Juicy

Fuji apples are sweet, crisp, and juicy, with a perfect balance of flavors, making them a delightful treat.
